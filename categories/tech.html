Tech Gadgets

Samsung

Samsung offers student discounts on a range of products, including smartphones, tablets, and laptops. Typically, students can save up to 10-20% off select items by verifying their student status through the Samsung Student Discount program.

Apple

Apple offers student discounts on a variety of products, including MacBooks, iPads, and more. Typically, students can save up to $100 on MacBooks and up to $50 on iPads, as well as enjoy special pricing on accessories through the Apple Education Store. Verification of student status is required to access these discounts.

Dell

Dell offers student discounts on a wide range of laptops, desktops, monitors, and accessories. Typically, students can save up to 10-20% off select items through the Dell Student Discount program by verifying their student status.

HP

HP offers student discounts on laptops, desktops, printers, and accessories. Typically, students can save up to 10-20% off select items through the HP Student Store by verifying their student status.

Adobe Creative Cloud

Adobe offers student discounts on its Creative Cloud subscription, which includes access to applications like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro. Typically, students can save up to 60-70% off the regular subscription price by verifying their student status through the Adobe Student Discount program.
